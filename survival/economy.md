# Economy

## ⓥ vibecoin

The main servers currency that is exchangeable between players. Use it for items, [community tasks](community-tasks.md), and try and reach the 10K cap!

### Making money

The best way to make vibecoin is by selling items to the [auction house](auction-house.md), the [server sell shop](server-sell-shop.md), [completing quests](questing.md), selling items to player's shops at the [market](market.md), winning [chat games](../general/rich-chat/chat-games.md), [voting](../general/misc./voting.md), and by making your own shop and selling items.

### Respawn fee

When you die, you will have to pay a **35%** respawn & keep inventory fee. High [VIP tiers ](../general/ranks/vip.md)can lower the fee down to **20%**. Currently if you have no vibecoin you will not pay any fee. There has been some conversation to potentially not keep inventory if you cannot pay any fee, but there is not any implementation as of current.

### Commands

* **`/bal [name]`**\
  See your or another players vibecoin balance\

* **`/pay <name> <amount>`**\
  Send vibecoin\

* **`/sales`**\
  View your recent chest shop (market) sale history
