# Economy

## ⓥ vibecoin

The main servers currency that is exchangeable between players. Use it for items, [community tasks](community-tasks.md), and try and reach the 10K cap! Each new player stats with a balance of 100 vibecoin.

### Making money

The best way to make vibecoin is by selling items to the [auction house](auction-house.md), the [server sell shop](server-sell-shop.md), [completing quests](questing.md), selling items to player's shops at the [market](market.md), winning [chat games](../general/rich-chat/chat-games.md), [voting](../general/misc./voting.md), and by making your own shop and selling items.

### Keep-inventory respawn fee

When you die, you will have to pay a **35%** respawn & keep inventory fee. High [VIP tiers ](../general/ranks/vip.md)can lower the fee down to **20%**. Currently if you have no vibecoin you will not pay any fee. If you are unable to pay the keep-inventory fee from your balance being below 1 vibecoin, your items will be dropped where you died

### Commands

* **`/bal [name]`**\
  See your or another players vibecoin balance\

* **`/pay <name> <amount>`**\
  Send vibecoin\

* **`/sales`**\
  View your recent chest shop (market) sale history
