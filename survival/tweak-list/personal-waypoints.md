# Personal waypoints

### Commands

* **`/sethome <name>`**\
  **``**Set a personal waypoint\
  _Limited to 5 for members,_ [_up to 32 for VIPs_](../../general/ranks/vip.md)__\
  __
* **`/home <name>`**\
  **``**Teleport back to a personal waypoint\

* **`/delhome <name>`**\
  **``**Delete a personal waypoint\

* **`/homes`**\
  See a list of your personal waypoints
