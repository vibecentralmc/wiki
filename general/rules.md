# Rules

{% hint style="info" %}
We have a set of rules everyone is expected to follow to ensure everybody has a safe and enjoyable experience on the server

These rules apply in server chat, item names, signs, books, builds, or any other content within the server. Applicable rules also apply to our [Discord ](discord.md)server.
{% endhint %}

## 1. Be kind & respectful

Any form of harassment or attack based on race, religion, sexuality, gender identity, or other aspects the make up individuals is not tolerated.\
\
Discriminatory, hateful, offensive speech or symbols of any kind are prohibited.

##

## 2. Content and chat should be kept PG**-**13

Without explicitly knowing otherwise, assume the members you’re speaking to are minors as the majority of the community is under 18.\
\
Triggering, dark, or similar topics that have potential to upset folk are not allowed (with exclusions to spoiler messages with proper trigger warnings in #vent on the Discord).\
\
Topics or language unsuitable for younger members should strictly be kept to private messaging so long as both parties are comfortable with the topic

##

## 3. Every member of the community has a voice

If someone asks you to stop doing something, change the topic, etc. politely honor their request. If somebody is made uncomfortable or upset by the current chat or situation, it’s every participating members job to adjust their behavior to accommodate

##

## 4. No politics

Making references to political topics or people, talking about serious issues involving government officials, political parties, religions, or geo-political disagreements is not allowed. The server is intended to be a safe space and a place for folk to escape. Our server or Discord is not the place for these kinds of discussions.

##

## 5. Nicknames must be genuine and appropriate&#x20;

Nicknames should be used as intended and be changed as minimally as possible. Please use a real nickname. Joke nicknames or nicknames with strong language or references to unsuitable topics are disallowed. Using nicknames to impersonate others, even as a joke is not allowed

##

## 6. Chat should be kept in English

For moderation purposes, please keep global chat in English. If you wish to speak in other languages, you may outside of global chat using private messaging commands, or by other means.

##

## 7. Ads unrelated to gameplay are not allowed

Acceptable advertisements include advertisements for members market shops, community waypoints, or other user created content on the server. However, all advertisements must not flood or spam chat.\
\
Detailed talk about non well-known servers may be considered a breach of this rule.

##

## 8. Griefing, stealing, or otherwise modifying anyone's builds or land, even unclaimed is not allowed unless you have explicit permission from the creator

Someone owns land when they have sufficiently modified the area, ex. through building a house, farm, or other meaningful structure. Naturally generated structures (villages, strongholds, ruined portals, desert temples, jungle temples, or witch huts), unless inhabited by another player are exempt from this rule.\
\
Modifying land within a radius of 500 blocks from someone's land may be considered violation of this rule. Though, common areas like the market are waived of this restriction.

Failing to respect the requests of public locations, ex. community farms asking you to replant, community chest at spawn asking to be mindful of what you take, etc. is considered a violation of this rule.

##

## 9. Personal builds like mines, houses, etc. should be kept outside of the market to allow the space to be used entirely for it's dedicated use of shops

Living in the market is not allowed. Any personal builds should be at least 500 blocks from the nearest shop in the market to allow for future market expansion

##

## 10. AFK rules

AFK farming for items in moderation is okay, but should never require any form of auto clicking or auto movement, including fish farms.

Excessive AFK durations / overnight afking is only allowed if not afk farming (excluding monthly jumps at survival spawn)

**Note that the server restarts daily at 3:55am EST**

##

## 11. Cheating of any form, abusing exploits, or using any client modifications that give you an advantage over users playing on vanilla is not allowed

Optimization mods (ex. Optifine or Sodium), and cosmetic mods (ex. Lunar, or Badlion clients) are allowed. The only exclusions to this rule is world maps, minimaps (without cave finders), and Litematica to assit helping you build by previewing schematics (without use of auto-building / printer). If you are unsure whether a specific game modification is allowed, just ask a staff member!

##

## 12. Intentionally overloading the server or impacting others fps and or connection in any form is prohibited

You may be asked to downsize large scale farms or redstone mechanisms if we find they disrupt the servers performance\


##

## 13. Messages that have excessive caps, repeat the same letter or word excessively, or alike flood chat and are not allowed

Chat has a variety of filters in place to ensure messages don’t disrupt others. Attempting to circumvent any filters by adding additional characters, or by other means, is prohibited.\


## 14. Circumvention of previously applied sanctions is prohibited

This includes but is not limited to:

* Joining on an alternate account after being banned or jailed
* Using signs, books, or other methods to speak while muted

##

## 15. Auction house auctions should be reasonably priced based on item rarity

Auctioning very common items in low quantities is not allowed Items of similar type should be bundled into a single auction instead of individually to prevent spam. Bundled auctions can be created using /ah sell (price) -b

##

## 16. Scamming or tricking other players out of items, vibecoin, or other belongings is not allowed

##

## 17. Engaging in pvp or otherwise damaging other players is not allowed unless all involved parties give permission

Having pvp toggled off does not count as permission

##

## 18. Market shops in the community market that consist of a sole or few chests are not allowed. The auction house should be used for selling of such items

Specific cases can be waived depending on item rarity or custom items (ex. special banner patterns), please ask a [staff member](ranks/#helper) if you are unsure if your case is in violation of this rule

##

## 19. The parkour course builder should be used in its intended fashion. Contents built in the course builder must be based around a parkour course

Courses may be deleted without notice, published or unpublished if we deem the feature is being misused. Publishing lots of very low-effort courses may be considered misuse.\
\
Using unpublished course builder spaces as 'private' creative worlds is disallowed. The dedicated [creative plotworld](../creative-plots/creative-plots.md) should be used instead.

##

## 20. Have good sportsmanship

While in a competitive environment on the server (ex. party games, the pvp arena, etc) it is expected you have good sportsmanship and are not toxic or rude toward others.

##

## 21. No teaming in the PvP arena or Party Games unless it is mutually agreed upon the whole lobby

Ensure everybody playing agrees that they're ok with teaming, else teaming is not allowed to ensure fairness.

##

## 22. Do not fabricate report details

All reports to staff of rule misconduct are expected to be legitimate. Intentionally fabricating or exaggerating report details is not allowed.
