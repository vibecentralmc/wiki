# Hub

The hub is the space between all gamemodes, and it's where newcomers first spawn. Largely hand built and packed with easter eggs and a whole cave system, it's a neat place to hang out & explore!

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/2023-09-03_02.31.43 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On the main hub platform, you can find two leaderboards: one containing the top 10 highest [leveled](../leveling.md) players, and one containing the 10 players with the highest playtime since the season reset. You can also find a cave with the name and skin of every Vibe [benefactor](../ranks.md#benefactor).
