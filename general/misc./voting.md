# Voting

Voting for vibe in the [PMC server list](https://www.planetminecraft.com/server/vibe-central/vote/) every 24 hours is an easy way for players to support the server and get rewards in the process!

### Rewards

* 50 [vibecoin](../../survival/economy.md)
* 150 [vibe experience](../leveling.md)

### Streaks

You can accumulate a vote streak by voting everyday. Your streak is announced in chat upon voting and announced in our [Discord](../discord.md) every acquired week.
